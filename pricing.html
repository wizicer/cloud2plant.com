It pays for itself

A value-added service

We encourage users to offset the cost of our service by charging clients a nominal monthly maintenance fee for their services. For example, many designers bill about $10 a month for a maintenance plan that includes the use of Surreal CMS.

We recommend offering your clients an all-in-one maintenance plan that includes:

  • Unlimited CMS access
  • Website analytics (via Google Analytics)
  • A limited number of design updates
  • Web hosting fees
  • Domain registration fees
  • Technical support

The details are entirely up to you, but as you can see, with just two clients on a maintenance plan you can actually profit from using Surreal—not to mention all the extra time you’ll have by letting clients safely edit their own content.

Cheaper than installables

By far, the biggest concern new users have is the recurring cost of our service. Although we make it as affordable as possible, some argue that Surreal will never be as cheap as installable alternatives.

However, there are many hidden costs to installable software—even free titles. We discuss some of these costs in this article.
