We’ve been nominated!

August 18, 2014

We received exciting news today: Surreal CMS has been nominated for two categories in the 2014 People’s Choice CMS Awards, hosted by CMS Critic!

A very special “thank you” goes out to everyone who voted for us. We’ve been nominated for the following categories:

  • Best Cloud CMS for small to midsize businesses
  • Best small to midsize business CMS (other languages)

So where do we go from here?

Voting starts today for the finals and will be held until October 17th. You don’t have to vote for every category (meaning you can skip the ones you’re not familiar with), but if you do, you’ll be eligible to win a new Chromebook courtesy of CMS Critic.

Are you as pumped about this as we are? Head over to cast your vote for Surreal CMS in both categories!

Thanks again for helping us make Surreal CMS the awesome service it is!
